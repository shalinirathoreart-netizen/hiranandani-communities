Hiranandani Parks, Oragadam (Chennai) – A Thriving IT Hub

Strategic Location & Infrastructure

Hiranandani Parks, located in Oragadam, is Chennai’s leading integrated township offering a perfect blend of work-life balance. Situated in the heart of Chennai’s industrial and IT corridor, it provides excellent connectivity through GST Road, two national highways, and the upcoming Greenfield Airport. The township is a preferred destination for IT professionals and corporate executives working in major business hubs nearby.

Integrated Township with Sustainable Living

Covering over 360+ acres, Hiranandani Parks is a fully developed, ready-to-move-in township offering:
✔ Spacious Villas, Villa Plots, and Apartments with clear titles and approvals.
✔ Green Living Spaces with over 2,000 trees, landscaped gardens, and jogging tracks.
✔ Plug-and-Play Infrastructure for hassle-free investment opportunities.

Lifestyle, Healthcare, and Education

✔ Thriveni Academy – A premier educational institution with 1,250+ students enrolled.
✔ 200-bed RELA Hospital – Offering top-tier medical facilities within the township.
✔ Sports & Recreation – A 9-hole golf course, cricket and football grounds, sports coliseum, and a massive clubhouse with an Olympic-sized swimming pool.

With its world-class infrastructure and growing employment opportunities, Hiranandani Parks is the perfect choice for IT professionals, corporate employees, and investors looking for long-term growth and high-quality living.

Hiranandani Fortune City, Panvel – A Smart & Sustainable Township

Prime Location & Future Growth

Strategically located in Panvel, Hiranandani Fortune City enjoys excellent connectivity to Mumbai and Pune through the Mumbai-Pune Expressway, upcoming Navi Mumbai International Airport, and Mohape & Panvel railway stations. The township is positioned as the next major residential and business hub due to massive infrastructure projects like the Mumbai Trans-Harbour Link and metro expansions.

A Smart Township Designed for Urban Living

✔ Offers a mix of studio, 1, 2, and 3 BHK apartments designed to cater to families, professionals, and investors.
✔ Self-sufficient township with residential, commercial, and retail spaces to promote a work-live-play lifestyle.
✔ Premium amenities, including business centers, daycare, yoga zones, coffee lounges, gymnasiums, swimming pools, and indoor games facilities.

Eco-Friendly & Sustainable Development

✔ Energy-efficient buildings integrated with rainwater harvesting and sewage treatment plants.
✔ Focus on green landscapes and sustainable urban planning.
✔ Educational & Retail Facilities – Hiranandani Trust School and operational high-street retail spaces for convenient urban living.

With its cutting-edge infrastructure, sustainable initiatives, and strategic location, Hiranandani Fortune City is an ideal choice for professionals and investors seeking futuristic urban living.

Hiranandani Gardens, Powai – The Gold Standard of Integrated Townships

A Visionary Transformation

Hiranandani Gardens, Powai, is a landmark development that transformed barren land into one of Mumbai’s most prestigious residential and commercial hubs. This iconic township set the benchmark for integrated urban living in India.

Luxury Living & Commercial Excellence

✔ Premium Residential Spaces – Luxurious apartments featuring state-of-the-art podium gardens, double-height entrance lobbies, and high-end fixtures.
✔ Work-Life Balance – Home to corporate offices, IT parks, and high-street retail, allowing professionals to live closer to their workplaces.
✔ Superior Lifestyle Amenities – Fitness centers, swimming pools, dedicated green spaces with walking tracks and parks.

Education & Healthcare at Your Doorstep

✔ Reputed Schools within the Township, making it ideal for families.
✔ World-Class Hiranandani Hospital, provides advanced healthcare services.
✔ Centrally located with seamless access to Mumbai’s business districts via well-planned roads and public transport.

Hiranandani Gardens, Powai, remains one of Mumbai’s most sought-after addresses, offering residents an unparalleled blend of luxury, convenience, and sustainability.
